Time spent being a Shuuenpro fan:

This webpage is dedicated to many (but not all) of the things I've done relating to Shuuen no Shiori.

Projects

Community-Related

Shuuen no Shiori Discord Server

MAR 19 2019 -> PRESENT
Unofficial Discord server for fans of Shuuen no Shiori, both old and new. 100+ members.

Week of Demise (Shuuen no Shiori Art Week)

SUMMER 2019 -> SUMMER 2021
SUMMER 2025 -> ?
An art week themed on Shuuen no Shiori held during the summer that uses the hashtag #weekofdemise. Now has a webpage for 2025-.

Shuuen no Shiori Bluesky Feed

JAN 9 2025 -> PRESENT
Bluesky feed that captures posts containing "shuuen no shiori", "shuuenpro", "#shuuennoshiori", "book(mark) of demise", "終焉ノ栞", and any of the main Shuuen no Shiori album names.

Coding

Shuuen Tally Counter

AUG 22 2020 -> PRESENT
Automated utility used to record statistics about how many times I mention "Shuuen" on any day. Has a Bluesky bot. Also has live-viewable graphs and tables.

30s Shuuen no Shiori Bot (Twitter)

FEB 15 2022 -> PRESENT
Posts trimmed clips of Shuuen no Shiori tracks every two hours.

E-ki Quote Bot (Bluesky)

JAN 6 2025 -> PRESENT
Posts quotes/lyrics said by E-ki. Hourly on Bluesky.

-Re꞉wind- 🔎🎵 (Discord Bot)

JAN 13 2025 -> PRESENT
A Discord bot that is designed with the ability to show album/song/track info and play over 100 music tracks from Shuuen no Shiori Project.

Hourly Film Club 🎞️ (Bluesky)

APR 12 2025 -> PRESENT
Posts quotes or images relating to the film club. Hourly on Bluesky.
