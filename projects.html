The S Counter / #namelessbot (Twitter Bot) - JAN 17 2022 -> PRESENT
Twitter bot that, every night, reports the number of times I said the words "Shuuen" or "Sonic" that day. Migrated from Google Sheets to SQLite3 database. More information, and the live hourly count can be found here.
Utilizes: Python (Tweepy), SQLite3, Raspberry Pi (cron job & bash script to upload and update live counter)

30s Shuuen Music (Twitter Bot) - FEB 15 2022 -> PRESENT
